Privacy Policy for Adashina

Last Updated: December 14, 2025

1. Introduction

Adashina ("we", "our", or "us") is a WhatsApp bot that helps manage Adashi rotating savings groups. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in an open and transparent manner.

2. Information We Collect

When you interact with Adashina, we collect:

  • Phone Number: Your WhatsApp phone number
  • Name: Your display name on WhatsApp
  • Contribution Records: Information about your Adashi contributions and payments
  • Messages: The content of messages you send to the bot for processing your requests

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Manage your Adashi group membership and contributions
  • Track contribution schedules and payment cycles
  • Send you reminders about upcoming contributions
  • Respond to your queries about your Adashi group
  • Maintain records of group transactions

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in Airtable and processed through Vercel servers. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is only shared with:

  • Airtable: For data storage and management
  • WhatsApp/Meta: For message delivery
  • Groq API: For processing natural language queries

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of the service at any time

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as you are an active member of an Adashi group. If you wish to have your data deleted, please contact us.
